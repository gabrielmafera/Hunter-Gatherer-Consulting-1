About Hunter Gatherer

Boutique Talent Acquisition Specialists Serving South Africa

Discover the difference.

Hunter Gatherer is a boutique talent acquisition and recruitment consultancy built on a shared passion for connecting exceptional people with outstanding organisations. Founded by Karin Van Antwerp and Amanda Peens, we bring decades of combined experience across South Africa's most dynamic sectors — from hospitality and security to logistics and professional services.

We believe recruitment is fundamentally a relationship business. That's why every engagement is led by one of our directors personally. Our deep understanding of the local business environment, combined with a rigorous, values-driven approach, ensures we deliver not just candidates — but lasting, transformative hires.
